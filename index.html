Cannabis Cultivation Data Products

poweredby.ci is a Loyal9 LLC data product providing verified, traceable cannabis cultivation intelligence. Under P.L. 119-37, signed November 12, 2025, the FY 2026 Agriculture Appropriations Act established a Total THC standard — Delta-9 THC plus THCA times 0.877 — fundamentally changing seed legality across the United States effective November 12, 2026. Our seed bank directory cross-references USDA active hemp licensees against state cannabis retail registries to identify dual-licensed bridge entities legally positioned to sell seeds after the law takes effect. Our strain database contains 21,220 verified cannabis strains with full genetics, breeder lineage, grow specifications, and 21,706 timestamped HTML source archives for complete traceability. Data products available for B2B licensing. Contact data@poweredby.ci.
